Detailed Description

The P_n(x) = (2n-1)/n * x * P_(n-1) - (n-1)/n * P_(n-2)

gives: A_n = (2*n-1)/n B_n = 0 C_n = (n-1)/n

References: [1] Golub, G.H., and Welsch, J.H. 1969, "Calculation of Gauss Quadrature Rules", Mathematics of Computation, vol. 23, pp. 221â€“230.
