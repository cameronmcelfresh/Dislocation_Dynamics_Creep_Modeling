For stress updating,

\[ \dot{\sigma}=\frac{\alpha}{1+\alpha} C::(\dot{\epsilon}-\dot{\epsilon}^p)+ \frac{1}{1+\alpha} \dot{\sigma} \]

\alpha=MachineStiffnessRatio, which is the stiffness ratio between machine stiffness and sample stiffness. More details see <Physical Review Letters 117, 155502, 2016>.
